How registration works
Need-based, in 5 fair steps
K

No click-race. You tell us what you need; the system allocates seats by demand score, not by who is fastest.

1

Submit your wishlist

Add & rank the courses you want within an open window. Order of submission never matters.
2

University sizes the classes

The school reviews real demand and opens as many sections as resources allow, then announces each class capacity.
3

Provisional slots by demand score

Seats are auto-assigned by each student's demand score. Over-capacity? You join the waitlist, ranked by that same score.
4

Confirm within 24h

Confirm your provisional classes. If you don't confirm or you drop one, the seat passes to the next student on the waitlist.
5

Cascade until the window closes

The system keeps re-allocating freed seats by priority — automatically — until registration ends.
⚖️
Every step is driven by need, transparency, and a fair waitlist — never speed or luck.
